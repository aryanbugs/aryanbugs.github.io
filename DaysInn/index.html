Stay with Us in Columbus GA

Near Columbus State University, Fort Benning, and Columbus airport.

Located off I-185, our Days Inn Columbus-North Fort Benning hotel, near Columbus State University, provides thoughtful amenities for a brighter stay. Our hotel in Columbus is just four miles from Fort Benning and Columbus Airport.

Energize Your Day

Enjoy perks like free breakfast, free WiFi, and our outdoor pool

Enjoy a free Daybreak continental breakfast, complete with hot items. Relax in the seasonal outdoor pool or check your email using our free WiFi from the comfort of your room. Each hotel room features a microwave and a refrigerator, and some rooms include 27-inch flat-screen TVs. We offer free parking and guest laundry facilities at our hotel.

Spend the Day Your Way

Visit a science museum, catch live performances, and enjoy a safari or car race

Spend the day at Columbus State University's Coca-Cola Space Science Center, less than five miles way. Browse the stores at Peachtree Mall and Columbus Park Crossing. Visit the Columbus Museum or check out the live performances at Springer Opera House. Catch car races at East Alabama Motor Speedway, just 15 miles away in Phoenix City. Enjoy an outdoor adventure at Wild Animal Safari, less than 40 miles away in Pine Mountain.
